How I Work

From raw data
to decisions

01
Understand the business first
No dashboard before understanding the operational problem. What decision does this data need to support? Who makes it and how often?
02
Build the data architecture right
Clean separation between data storage and analysis. Star schema, correct granularity, documented business logic. The foundation determines everything.
03
Let the data challenge assumptions
The most valuable insights are the ones that contradict what management thinks they already know. Build for discovery, not just confirmation.
